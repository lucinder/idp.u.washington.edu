Your University of Washington student account shows an outstanding balance. To avoid late payment fees and a hold on your academic record, further action is needed.

You may view and pay your tuition & fees online using MyUW at https://my.uw.edu/accounts/.   • For complete statement and payment instructions, visit https://www.degreereg.uw.edu/payment-procedures
  • To go directly to the PCE-Continuum College payment portal, visit https://portal.continuum.uw.edu
